The Books

The Secret History

Written by Donna Tart. Originally published in 1992 by Alfred A. Knopf.

Six college students-- a rich guy, a rich gay guy, two rich twin people, another rich person that keeps taking their money, and a normal person on financial aid-- find themselves taken in by the teachings of the elusive Greek professor, Julian. After one thing leads to another, secret rituals lead to the group getting involved in a murder-- first of an innocent passersby, and later, one of their very own: Bunny.

note: there are no other books in this series
