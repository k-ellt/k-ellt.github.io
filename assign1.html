The Secret History

Six college students-- a rich guy, a rich gay guy, two rich twin people, another rich person that keeps taking their money, and a normal person on financial aid-- find themselves taken in by the teachings of the elusive Greek professor, Julian. After one thing leads to another, secret rituals lead to the group getting involved in a murder-- first of an innocent passersby, and later, on eof their very own: Bunny.

The victim and perpetrators are known from the first page. Unlike typical murder stories, The Secret History explore more of the events that led up to the murder, and then the aftermath. Honestly, I just liked the way the words flowed. Chill vibes, love the way their sanity started crumbling-- absolutely amazing. I think Henry was pretty cool, and Francis. Richard was alright, poor guy was kinda traumatized. Kinda funny.
